#  Champs Pizza Website

Welcome to **Champs Pizza**, a modern and fully responsive WordPress website for a pizza restaurant located in Sterling, VA. This project highlights handcrafted pizzas, premium toppings, and a wide variety of subs, salads, wings, and more all designed to deliver an engaging, smooth, and user-friendly online ordering experience for customers.

---
##  Live Demo

Check out the live site: [Champs Pizza Website](https://champspizzava.com/)

---

##  Features

- Fully responsive design optimized for mobile and desktop
- Interactive pizza menu with categories: pizzas, calzones, subs, wings, salads, sides, wraps, burgers, and rice bowls
- Online ordering integration ([Order Online](https://www.orderchampspizzamenu.com))
- Customizable homepage sections: Featured items, Specials, Best Sellers
- Contact form with Google Maps integration
- Newsletter subscription and social media links
- SEO optimized for local search

---



##  Menu Highlights

### Pizzas
- Cheese Pizza, Pepperoni Pizza, Champs Tandoori Pizza  
- Supreme Meat, Buffalo Chicken, Grand Slam, Chicken Fajita, Philly Cheese Steak  
- Hawaiian, BBQ Chicken, Gyro, Vegetarian, Greek, Cauliflower (Gluten-Free)  

### Specials & Combos
- Family Special: 2 large pizzas, 24 wings  
- Buddy Deal: 1 large pizza, 16 wings, 2L soda  
- BFF Deal: 1 medium pizza, 8 wings, 2 cans of soda  

### Subs, Wraps & Burgers
- Philly Cheesesteak, Steak & Cheese, BBQ Chicken, Tandoori Chicken Subs  
- Crispy Chicken Wrap, Grilled Chicken Wrap, Tandoori Wrap  
- Champs Cheeseburger, Bacon Cheese Burger, Fish Burger  

### Salads & Sides
- Tandoori Chicken Salad, Caesar Salad, Greek Salad  
- French Fries, Cajun Fries, Cheese Fries, Garlic Bread, Onion Rings, Chicken Tenders  

### Extras & Sauces
- Ranch, Balsamic, Champ Sauce, Honey Mustard, BBQ, Marinara, Tzatziki, Caesar Dressing
